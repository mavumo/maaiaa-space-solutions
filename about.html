About the company

At Maaiaa Space Solutions, we reimagine the possibilities of modern living and working spaces. Specializing in commercial and residential developments crafted from steel containers and modular furniture, we create innovative, sustainable, and cost-effective solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our expertise lies in transforming repurposed steel containers into stylish, durable, and versatile structures that blend form with function. From contemporary office spaces and pop-up retail stores to custom-designed homes and eco-friendly community hubs, our modular approach ensures maximum efficiency without compromising on quality or aesthetics.

We are fully committed to:

• Sustainability: Using recycled materials and eco-conscious practices to reduce our environmental footprint.

• Customisation: Offering fully customisable designs that adapt to your vision, space, and lifestyle.

• Affordability: Providing innovative solutions that are both practical and budget-friendly.
• Speed & Efficiency: Accelerating project timelines with our modular construction techniques, delivering results faster than traditional methods.
